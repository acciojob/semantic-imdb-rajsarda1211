Plot Summary

Inception is a science fiction movie about a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who enters the subconscious minds of his targets to steal their secrets. The movie explores complex themes of dreams, reality, and the nature of the human mind as Cobb is tasked with planting an idea into someone's subconscious, a process called "Inception."
